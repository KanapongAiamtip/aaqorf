A ripe pomelo is typically orange in color, with a slightly dimpled or bumpy texture on its skin. When you press your fingers gently onto the surface, it should yield slightly, indicating that it's soft and juicy inside. The fruit has a sweet, fragrant aroma, and when you peel it, you'll find succulent, juicy, and vibrant pomelo segments that burst with flavor when you bite into them.

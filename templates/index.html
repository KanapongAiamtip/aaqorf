Origin and Significance

This project has arisen from the need to assess and analyze the quality of pomelo fruits

in the process of selecting high-quality produce. Checking the completeness of the produce is of great importance as incomplete fruits can have an impact on the quality and value of the produce. Utilizing image processing technology can help in swiftly and accurately analyzing the completeness of oranges, thus enhancing efficiency in decision-making regarding the management and selection of produce at each stage of the production process. Researchers have embarked on this study to make it easier for farmers to calculate the completeness of pomelo.
